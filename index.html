Support

Getting a blue
screen warning?

Completely normal. Windows shows this for every unsigned app from an indie developer. Here’s exactly what’s happening and how to get past it in 10 seconds.

1
Why it happens
Microsoft charges ~$300/year for code-signing certificates. Editor's Stash is completely free, so it's unsigned. Windows flags all unsigned apps by default — safe or not.
2
Click "More info"
When the blue SmartScreen dialog appears, don't close it. Click "More info" in the middle of the dialog.
3
Click "Run anyway"
A "Run anyway" button appears at the bottom. Click it. App opens normally — you'll never see this again for this version.
4
Is it safe?
Yes. Runs 100% offline. Zero background network requests. Only connects when you explicitly paste a URL to download something.
